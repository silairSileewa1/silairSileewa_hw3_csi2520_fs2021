Stephen curry is the greatest shooter of all time. recently he passed ray Allen in career three pointers made, and holds the record for most three pointers in a season with a mind boggling 402 three pointers in one season alone. Stephen curry is on his way to his 3rd MVP which only 8 other players have accomplished in NBA history. he joins elite company in Michael Jordan, LeBron James, wilt Chamberlin, Moses Malone, magic Johnson, Larry bird, Kareem Abdul Jabbar and bill Russell. In this video curry torches the Chicago bulls, the current number 1 seed in the eastern conference for 40 points on a great shooting percentage, adding to his hot streak and solidifying his case for MVP this season.
